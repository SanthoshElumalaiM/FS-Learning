Preparing Burgars

Prep Time: 15 Mins

Cook Time: 20 Mins

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients And Seasonings
  2. Mix the Burger Patties
  3. Shape the Patties
  4. Preheat the Gril or Skillet
  5. Cook the Patties
  6. Toast the Buns
  7. Assemble the Burgers
  8. Serve the Burgers

Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity
Burger Patties 500 Mg
Buns 4
Tomato slices 5
Onion slices 6
